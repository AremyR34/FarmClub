🌱 Free Community Farm Days · Oceanside

Welcome to The Backyard Farm Club

If a day at the zoo or a family outing just isn't in the budget right now, this one's for you. Once or twice a month, we open up a real backyard farm in North County San Diego so kids can feed animals, dig in the garden, and make something with their hands — completely free.
