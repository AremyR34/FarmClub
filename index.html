🌱 Free Community Farm Days · Oceanside

Welcome to The Backyard Farm Club

If a day at the zoo or a family outing just isn't in the budget right now, this one's for you. Once or twice a month, we open up a real backyard farm in North County San Diego so kids can feed animals, dig in the garden, and make something with their hands — completely free.

What Happens at a Farm Day

Every farm day is a little different, but there's always something to feed, something to plant, and something to make.

🐰 Meet the Animals

Kids get up close with our farm friends — feeding, gentle petting, and learning what it takes to care for another living thing.

👧 All ages welcome 🕒 Farm days run 10:00am–1:00pm
  • Feeding animals
  • Collecting eggs
  • Gentle grooming
  • Animal observation

🌿 Explore the Garden

From seed to sprout, kids discover where food comes from and how things grow.

👧 All ages welcome 🕒 Farm days run 10:00am–1:00pm
  • Planting seeds
  • Watering & weeding
  • Bug hunting
  • Harvest tasting

🎨 Create & Play

Nature-inspired arts and crafts stations where kids can slow down and make something to take home.

👧 All ages welcome 🕒 Farm days run 10:00am–1:00pm
  • Nature crafts
  • Painting
  • Garden art
  • Free play

Farm days are designed for children of all ages, and siblings are always welcome. Activities are hands-on, kid-paced, and led with plenty of adult support.

📅 Held once or twice a month — Saturdays in North County San Diego
