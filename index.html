About The Backyard Farm Club

The Backyard Farm Club started with two nannies, a friend's backyard farm, and a simple idea: kids light up around animals and gardens, and that experience shouldn't depend on what a family can afford.

After years of working with children, we saw how few kids in our community ever get to feed a chicken, plant a seed, or watch something grow. Outings like the zoo keep getting more expensive, and for a lot of families right now, they're simply out of reach.

So once or twice a month, we open up the farm and invite families to spend the day with us — free, no strings attached. Kids explore, create, and care for animals. Parents get a morning of connection and community. And everyone leaves a little more grounded than they arrived.

🎯 Our Mission

To give North County families free, hands-on farm experiences where children can explore nature, care for animals, and grow their curiosity — together.

💡 How It Works

We host farm days once or twice a month at a backyard farm in Oceanside. Families reserve a free spot, and we handle the rest — activities, animal time, and crafts included.

❤️ Our Values

Trust, community, and joy. We don't ask anyone to prove they need this. We just ask that you come ready to get a little dirt under your nails.
